Bathymetry Graph

  • Click the 'Data entry template' link, and make a copy of the template spreadsheet.
  • Fill in your data.
  • Go to share options and change the sharing settings to 'Anyone with the link.'
  • Copy the URL for your spreadsheet, and paste it in the 'Spreadsheet URL' field.
  • Click 'Generate Graph.'
  • Note: if your graph isn't loading, check to make sure you set the sharing settings to 'Anyone with the link.'
